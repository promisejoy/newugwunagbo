Planning Department

Shaping the future of Ugwunagbo through strategic planning, sustainable development initiatives, and coordinated project implementation.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To make Ugwunagbo LGA a model of sustainable development through innovative planning and effective project implementation.

Our Mission

To provide strategic direction, coordinate development projects, and ensure sustainable growth through participatory planning processes.

Building Plan Approval Process

  1. Submit completed application form
  2. Provide survey plan and architectural drawings
  3. Pay processing fees at Finance Department
  4. Site inspection by planning officers
  5. Approval within 21 working days
